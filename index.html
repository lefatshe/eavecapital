About Us THEORY is an outcome of EXPERIENCE, and that is the basis of our philosophy. Investment

Eave Capital is entrepreneurial by nature. Our dedicated investment business focuses on high growth entrepreneurial businesses looking for expansion or considering equity partnerships.

We actively pursue feasible investment opportunities, across selected sectors. Our current position reflects the sound execution of our business strategy, centered on growth equity investments. Eave Capital operates an investment holding business, holding its listed and unlisted investments. Our strategy to invest directly into businesses supports our vision, by utilizing proceeds to further strengthen our balance sheet.

Our professionals understand that company value is built primarily through business growth, and will only venture into those businesses where they are confident of being able to add real value and to help our investee companies achieve this.

We seek partnerships with the companies in which we invest, sharing the same risks and rewards as other shareholders for time frames of several years or more. It is entirely this conviction that underlines our philosophy to be actively and operationally involved in our underlying investments.

Investment Strategy

Our strategic investments are investments that complement our operations and offer growth opportunities. We seek investments in the small and medium-sized enterprises’ space, and our strategic intent is to invest in opportunities that will present:
  • Consistent positive cash flows;
  • Long-term real Return on Capital; and
  • Sustainable long-term growth and development prospects.

Investment Role

Our investment professionals bring a wealth of financial knowledge and investment savvy. As Eave Capital, our role to our investment partners includes:
  • Strategy Direction
  • New Market Development
  • Leveraged Capital Expansion
  • Governance and Compliance
Empowerment

Management’s commitment to empowerment is holistic, aiming to incorporate the South African government’s current Black Economic Empowerment objectives, as well as further the wellbeing and overall development of our communities.

The advancement of empowerment and transformation is a cornerstone to the long-term sustainability of our operations. We remain committed at all times to sustainable Corporate Social Investment and development.

Corporate Governance

Our management possesses great skills and knowledge in corporate governance, through both formal education and practice. Corporate Governance is placed as a core priority in ensuring transparency; and it is the framework that underpins our operations.
